Welcome to the Central Wyoming College Computer Tech Website

Why Choose Us

  • Expert Faculty: Our distinguished faculty members bring a wealth of real-world experience and industry insights to the classroom, ensuring you receive a top-notch education.
  • Cutting-Edge Curriculum: Stay at the forefront of technology with our innovative and up-to-date curriculum, designed to equip you with the skills demanded by today's tech-driven world.
  • Hands-On Learning: Gain practical experience through hands-on projects, internships, and research opportunities, preparing you for success in the tech industry.
  • Career Support: Our dedicated career services team provides guidance, networking opportunities, and job placement assistance to help you launch your dream career.

Computer Science Program

  • Software Design Focus: The Computer Science program is designed for students passionate about computer software design. You'll explore methods for formulating and solving complex problems using current techniques, skills, and tools essential for the field of computing.
  • Diverse Computing Topics: Beyond traditional programming, our curriculum covers a wide range of topics, including network analysis, graphics, modeling and simulation, parallel computation, and artificial intelligence. You'll gain exposure to diverse areas within computer science.
  • Problem-Solving Skills: Develop strong problem-solving skills that are highly sought after in the technology industry. You'll learn to tackle complex challenges and create innovative solutions.
  • Hands-On Projects: Engage in hands-on projects and practical applications of computer science concepts. These experiences allow you to apply your knowledge and creativity to real-world problems (like this website).
  • Preparation for the Future: Equip yourself with the skills and expertise needed to excel in the dynamic world of technology. As a Computer Science graduate, you'll be prepared to take on roles in software development, data analysis, research, and more.

Computer Technology Program

  • Comprehensive IT Foundations: Our Computer Technology program introduces you to fundamental hardware, software, networking, and security principles, providing a solid foundation for your IT career.
  • Certification Opportunities: Prepare for industry-recognized certifications such as CompTIA A+™ hardware certification, CompTIA Network+™, and CompTIA Security+™. These certifications enhance your qualifications and job prospects in the IT field.
  • Broad Knowledge: Gain a well-rounded understanding of computer networking, hardware, and software, along with valuable insights into the Linux operating system. This diverse knowledge equips you for various IT roles.
  • Hands-On Experience: Put your classroom knowledge into practice through on-the-job work during an internship. This hands-on experience allows you to apply the skills you've acquired and build your confidence in real-world IT scenarios.
  • Career Opportunities: Graduates of our Computer Technology program have pursued successful careers as network engineers, computer hardware technicians, information systems help desk professionals, and network administrators.
