College Life

Living on Campus

Living on campus offers you the chance to embrace independence while immersing yourself in a tight-knit community. Here are some key benefits:

  • Social Opportunities: Engage in social activities, meet diverse individuals, and participate in a wide range of campus events.
  • Cozy Campus: Enjoy the intimate, accessible, and secure environment at CWC, where you can attend events like "Casino Night" or play games with fellow residents.
  • Cultural Exchange: Learn about different cultures by interacting with peers from across Wyoming and around the world.

Living on campus at CWC offers a unique blend of independence, community, and a vibrant social experience.

Campus Events

Get ready for an exciting lineup of campus events! From gatherings that bring students together to celebrate, socialize, and have a great time, there's always something happening on campus to keep your social calendar brimming with fun and memorable moments. Join us for unforgettable experiences and make memories that will last a lifetime.

  • Movie Nights: Join us for cinematic adventures under the stars or in our cozy indoor settings.
  • Pizza Parties: Savor delicious slices and connect with fellow pizza enthusiasts at our pizza extravaganzas.
  • Bonfires: Gather around the crackling bonfire, toast marshmallows, and share stories with friends old and new.
  • And More: Stay tuned for a wide variety of other exciting events and surprises throughout the year!

Clubs & Organizations

Student organizations are the heartbeat of connection, the pulse of fun, and the breeding ground for discovery, leadership, and creativity. When you join a club or organization, you're not just signing up; you're embarking on a memorable journey filled with fantastic connections waiting to be made.

  • Agriculture Club: Dive into the world of agriculture, connect with fellow enthusiasts, and cultivate your passion for farming and agribusiness.
  • Criminal Justice Club: Join a group of aspiring crime solvers and legal minds to explore the world of criminal justice, law enforcement, and public safety.
  • Multi-Cultural Club: Embrace diversity and foster cultural understanding by becoming a part of our multi-cultural community, where you can celebrate different traditions and perspectives.

Competitive Teams

At CWC, we offer a diverse range of athletics teams to cater to your sports interests. Join any of our competitive teams and become a part of the action.

  • Men/Women's Basketball: Join our competitive basketball teams and be a part of the exciting action on the court.
  • Men/Women's Soccer: Lace up your cleats and join our soccer teams to showcase your skills on the field.
  • Golf: Tee off with our golf team and master your swing while representing CWC.
  • Volleyball: Spike, set, and serve your way to victory as a member of our dynamic volleyball team.
  • E-Sports: Dive into the world of competitive gaming with our E-Sports team.
